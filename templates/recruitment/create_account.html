What is a Prospectives Account?

A Prospectives Account allows you to keep track of the point system. What you can do with your account:

  • Sign up for sophomore meals.
  • Sign up for our club wide events.
  • Keep track of how many points you have
When you become a member, your account will be upgraded to a full member account. Your account will also allow you to view parts of the websitess that are for prospectives only!

Who should make one?

The prospectives account should only be made for sophomores. Freshmen cannot join eating clubs until their sophomore year. Upperclassmen can join directly (email one of our officers).

How do I make one?

Just fill out the form below, and we'll use Princeton CAS system to create an account for you our database.

On behalf of our undergraduate membership and alumni body, let me be the first to welcome you to Charter. Since 1901, undergraduates have joined Charter to be part of one of the friendliest and most dynamic social communities at Princeton. Charter provides an exceptional level of service to its members. We have a beautiful clubhouse filled with amenities like our TV room, our computer cluster equipped with several multi-screen desktops (excellent for serious work or serious gaming), our big backyard, and much more. Add in our delicious and diverse dining selections, as well as our packed social calendar, and it's easy to see why the quality of the Charter experience is unrivaled on the Street. All of this ensures the bonds formed at Charter last lifetimes, which is why alumni come back in large numbers for football games and Reunions.

Best Regards,

Jean-Carlos (JC) Arenas, Class of 2016
Undergraduate President
